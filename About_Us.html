About Us

Scroll to get to know minds behind CoreVision

Prabhas Kumar

Hello, I am Prabhas Kumar. My journey into technology began with a fascination for electronics and an insatiable curiosity. As a child, I tinkered with toy cars and scavenged for electronic parts, which quickly evolved into a passion for microcontrollers and drones. By 6th grade, I was delving into the world of PCB design and robotics, driven by the excitement of creating something new. My first introduction to programming was with Visual Basic, sparking a belief in my ability to create software. This led me to Arduino and C++, where I further honed my skills. Despite the challenges of an education system that often felt restrictive, I pursued my interests relentlessly. I immersed myself in AI and Python, recognizing the potential of these technologies early on. My journey took a significant turn when I met Aksh Singh during my 12th grade. Our shared vision and complementary skills led us to co-found CoreVision, with the mission to revolutionize 3D design and computation. Together, we are developing NexusCAD and VisionCAD, aiming to make advanced design tools accessible and immersive for all creators.

Aksh Singh

Hello, I am Aksh Singh. I’ve always been driven by a relentless curiosity for technology. It began with scavenging electronics and modifying toy cars, evolving into mastering microcontrollers and drones. By 9th grade, my passion led me to design high-altitude rockets, employing advanced CAD modeling, fluid dynamics, guidance systems, and return-to-launch capabilities. Along the way, I interacted with visionary leaders like Tory Bruno, CEO of ULA, and absorbed insights from MIT graduates. Realizing the need for more intuitive design tools, I co-founded CoreVision with my friend, Prabhas Kumar. Our mission is simple yet profound: to revolutionize how creators interact with their designs, making sophisticated technology accessible and immersive.
